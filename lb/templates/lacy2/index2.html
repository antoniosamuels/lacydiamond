Kansas City birthed me, Arkansas raised me and I became an entertainer in Columbia South Carolina,” quotes Lacy Diamond. Lacy Diamond was born in Kansas City, Missouri and moved to a small town in the Northeast corner of Arkansas called Blytheville when he was in middle school. While in Blytheville Lacy focused on basketball, which took him to Columbia, South Carolina as he earned a scholarship to play for Benedict College.
